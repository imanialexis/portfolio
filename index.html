About Me

I'm a full stack Software Engineer based in New York, NY. Originally from Miami, I moved to New York in 2014 for more opportunities and the desire to contribute to one of the most competitive hubs of innovation in the country. Before I became a Software Engineer, I was an Executive Assistant and Account Executive in Investment Banking and Real Estate respectively. In both industries, I excelled specifically when it came to leveraging technologies to make my job more efficient and quite frankly, easier. What really inspired me to make the transition to tech were the endless possibilites for innovation. Innovation is what drives me and my goal is work with a company that encourages people to rethink the possibilities of digital communication, e-commerce and civic responsibility.


Much like the risk I took 2014 years ago moving to New York, I decided to resign from my current job in Investment Banking and pursue programming full time. It was the most challenging, yet most rewarding thing I've even done to date. Since then,I've been able to build some pretty cool projects. Check them out below and stay tuned for more!
